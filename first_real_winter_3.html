Okay so, I'm back to Bangalore, it's around 1100 hours, sunny and hot, Arpan's texting somebody, you know who.

I'm now practically done with college, and I can't believe it. Thanks to everybody whom I met on campus, it was a fun ride.

Getting back to business, this one is gonna be on the remaining month of December, 2022. I'm kinda confused where do I start? Okay, let's do IISM first.

IISERB hosted the IISM 2022, and it was my first month there. Absolutely goated stuff. Also, the FIFA WC finals were this month, again, banger timing. Coming to IISM, it's a crazy experience, both as a player and spectator. Talking of spectatorship, IISERB tops out on the experience. We are probably the most passionate players as well as spectators, the hooting was unreal, the lines were impeccably abusive, amazing. I still remember how we used to bunk classes to go watch the kabaddi matches, Anoushka and Rashi should remember. I met Yash (Choudhary) during this time, through Ano, Rohit as well. Anjuman was another one from the Enthuzia team I met, from 19 batch. Gosh, it was so fun back then. We were divided into teams of 'Associates' for PR, Soc Med, etc, etc. Gupta was in the Soc Med team. I don't know if he remembers, or if Rakshith remembers, we both were interviewed at Crescent at the small opening of sorts between the two staircase complexes, late in the night, after which we both went to eat Tayyab rolls. Veg Roll, 70 Rs, one by two. We were blown away by how good they were, really.

Even though there's a lot of negativity around Enthuzia'23, I still regard it as one of the best experiences I had at IISERB, majorly because everybody I met through this one thing was really amazing, all seniors, and batchmates. It was just, fun and nice. You hardly can hate on things which got you pretty people even though the things themselves were shit. Nilu, who worked alongside me in the PR team, was another interesting ass I met. Also, he was so gol matol back then. I did meet Aarya (Gupta) but hardly had much interaction, she was damn introverted back then.

I'm also really sorry for the delay on this one, had been tightly busy with too much work. Managing my internship and thesis was just really hard. Now that I'm free of one, I do plan to write down more regularly, also thinking of starting one on my school life. Let's see how that goes.
