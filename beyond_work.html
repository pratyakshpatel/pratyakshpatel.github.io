Beyond Work

Blog

Like others, I wasn't blessed with any artistic talent like singing, dancing, or music; however, I was lucky enough to discover that I do share some passion for writing — be it technical, or just letting my thoughts out on a piece of paper. I have been writing for the science magazine of IISERB, Chrysalis, for some time, and also run a blog of my own, where I write about my life. I'm also planning to start a technical blog and share some of the crazy stuff I come across in research papers or even random Reddit threads.

Reading

The passion for writing wasn't just a sudden occurrence — it was a consequence of my reading habit as a kid. Though I've never read fiction to date, I've read countless biographies and autobiographies. Lately, I've also developed a taste for history, especially modern history and war narratives.
